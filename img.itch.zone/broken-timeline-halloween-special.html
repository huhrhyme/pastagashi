Broken Timeline - Halloween Special

A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, and Linux

You've been invited to a party by your friend Pete.

Accompanied by your friends Ken and Kym, you can chat with the party guests and just enjoy the time. But something is hiding in the dark, watching your every move... Can you survive the party?

HALLOWEEN SPECIAL is a short story that precedes the main story of BROKEN TIMELINE, which will be available this December.

-Star Gamers Productions
