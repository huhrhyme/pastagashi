Broken Timeline - Prologue

A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, and Linux

You have been invited... To witness the origin of a friendship!

In this prologue, you're invited to a party by your friend Pete. Talk, drink and get comfy!

BROKEN TIMELINE PROLOGUE is just a pinch of what you can expect from the main game. Divided into 5 exciting chapters, you will meet multiple characters and can help them with their emotional problems. The story will continue later this year.

- Star Gamers Productions
