SODSUD

A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, and Linux

     GAME STORY

Welcome to the Underworld, you got recruited by the SODSUD General Association to work as a store clerk. We saw your life experience and we think you are the perfect candidate to work in the SODSUD convenience store.

Your story starts one week before winter, the Underworld is getting colder every day and life down in there is not getting any easier.


     ABOUT THE GAME

SODSUD is a game by SightCel from Papeero set in the world of the dead, you play as a store clerk new to the Underworld, are you ready to meet the interesting residents of this world?

The game is made out of handmade clay figures with stop motion animations like this one:


The game consist of 7 days before the Underworld residents hibernate, 30 original characters, 30 original products and 13 animals you can interact with.

Winter is coming and people are desperate for your products. Be sure to get your sweater on because this winter will be colder than ever.

-SightCel

Oxymoronically1 year ago

The game was good. The graphics, interesting characters, witty-named items, and the little things you could interact with all made it a pleasant experience. The game-play got kind of bland after day five or so, though. I think adding in more cool little events like the robbery would've helped avoid dullness. ( The other  stuff like Chloe getting a job as a news reporter, a man selling masks, and the lady missing her head, I don't consider an event.) On the seventh day, I was honestly expecting some grand finale or weird plot twist of sorts. Maybe something to do with the  "Mortis" near the end, but to my ironic surprise, nothing crazy happened. The part where you could choose five products for yourself was a clever, fun little addition. Besides that, I was a bit disappointed with how it ended. The game itself has a ton of potential. It mainly just needs some tweaking plot-wise. 

Other mentions:

-Good music

-Interesting lore

-Perfect walking-speed 

-Great character designs

All in all, if I had to rate the game 1/10, I'd give it a 7/10.
